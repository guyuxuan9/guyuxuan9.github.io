Matplotlib 2

Posted on 2020-04-28 | In Data analysis

Today, I have looked at some methods of matplotlib.

.pairplot()

First of all, the data is loaded. This data is about the sepal widths, sepal length, petal width and petal length of 3 different kinds of Iris Flower.

The data of tips picked is loaded.

First of all, a new column in the tips dataFrame is added, called tip_pct which is calculated to represent the proportion of tips of the total bill paid. Then, 'sex' and 'time' columns are picked to represent the x axis and y axis respectively. bins=np.linspace(0,40,15) means the x axis starts from 0 and ends at 40, with 15 equal intervals. As shown in the graph, Male pays more bill at dinner. Maybe it is due to the fact that males like to treat others or invite others and have a party at dinner.

.factorplot()

Factor plot can be used to show the distribution of one parameter in another parameter, such as the distribution of total bill in each day.

Here, kind='reg' means regression, so the linear regression is automatically done. The univariate graphs are shown at the top and right of the graph, and the bivariate graph is shown at the centre. The shaded area represents the loss of the regression.
