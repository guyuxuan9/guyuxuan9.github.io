Posted on 2020-04-25 | In Data analysis |

Today, I have learnt some useful methods about the matplotlib.

Area and population in California City

<Image from https://ontheworldmap.com/usa/state/california/map-of-california-max.jpg>

First of all, the csv file is loaded by pd.read_csv().

The lattitude and longitude of the city and its corresponding area and population is the interested data, so they are selected from the file.

Scattered graph is used here. The x-axis stands for the longitude of the city and the y-axis stands for the lattitude of the city. Different colors are used to represent different number (density) of population. As the population is too large, the log scale is used to represent the large number. s is the size of the point on the scattered diagrame which represents the area of the city. cmap is the parameter used to set the type of colormap, such as ‘binary’ and ‘viridis’.

Here, the scale used in the x-axis and y-axis is the same. What is more, areas of 100, 300, 500 are used to give an approximation of the relation between the size of the circle and the area. Note that in line 11, x value and y value are none, so the legend is set using labels instead of x,y values, so plt.legend() must be used to show the legend. Otherwize, the legend will not be shown.

The graph shows the distribution of area and population from North-west to South-east in California.

2D Histogram

The Mulrivariate Gaussian Distribution is used to generate the x-axis and y-axis of the graph. bins parameter is used to define the number of equal-width bins in the range.

This diagrame has very important applications in my opinion. For example, in a shop, the enterprise can have an idea of the popularity of different goods by looking at the density of people in that area. This can be achieved by using an infrared detector which detects the heat emitted from human beings. The darker the color, the more people there are in that area. In doing so, the enterprise can adjust the supply of different products according to different demand to maximise their profit.
